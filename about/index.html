Reena Harkishnani is a premier licensed real estate broker with HomeSmart Real Estate Associates. With deep roots in the Pacific Northwest, she has built a reputation for her relentless work ethic and her ability to go above and beyond for her clients.

Specializing in the Indian community across the Greater Seattle Area, Reena provides professional services in both Hindi and English. She understands that buying a home is a cultural and emotional milestone, often requiring specific expertise in Vastu-compliant layouts and proximity to community hubs.

From helping first-time homebuyers secure their first keys to advising seasoned investors on high-ROI property flips, Reena's diverse background and years of expertise make her an invaluable partner in any transaction.
